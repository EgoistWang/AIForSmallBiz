The Problem

Small manufacturers face steep engineering costs, scarce automation talent, and regulatory hurdles. As a result, manual processes persist, production uptime suffers, and opportunities for growth are missed.

  • High Engineering Costs: Custom PLC/HMI solutions can run into tens of thousands of dollars.
  • Skills Gap: Recruiting or contracting controls engineers is out of reach for many small firms.
  • Compliance Risks: Failing to meet ISA‑88, FDA or OSHA standards can result in costly fines.
