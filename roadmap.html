Implementation Roadmap

From concept to full‑scale rollout, see how AImpact4SmallBiz will reach 500+ customers in 36 months.

  1. Phase 1 Months 1‑6

    Development

    Build the AI engine, secure cloud infrastructure (AWS), and release the first mobile app. Develop 10 initial templates (5 for water purification, 5 for medical devices), compliance tools for EPA and FDA standards, and 5 training modules. Conduct beta testing with 10 businesses to refine functionality and ensure compliance.

    Key Activities:
    • Platform architecture design and AWS setup
    • AI engine development using TensorFlow
    • UI/UX for web and mobile (React Native)
    • Security: encryption, role-based access
    • Initial regulatory compliance tools
    • $30,000 for software/server, $10,000 for content, $5,000 for testing
    Milestone: Platform MVP, 10 templates, 5 modules, 10 beta users

    Learn More
  2. Phase 2 Months 7‑12

    Pilot Testing

    Launch pilot programs with 5–10 small businesses, offering free access for 6 months to demonstrate ROI (e.g., 60% cost reduction, 30% downtime reduction). Gather feedback, optimize features, and secure $15,000 in grants and $10,000 in supplier fees. Begin digital marketing and industry outreach.

    Key Activities:
    • Pilot onboarding, user training, and support
    • Collect feedback, usage data, and ROI metrics
    • Refine compliance modules and templates
    • Establish supplier partnerships
    • Launch digital marketing and outreach
    Milestone: 5–10 pilot users, $25,000 in funding, validated product-market fit

    Learn More
  3. Phase 3 Months 13‑18

    Market Launch

    Public subscription release with tiered plans. Onboard first 100 paying customers, expand content (5 new templates, 3 new training modules), and launch marketing campaigns (SEO, social media, webinars). Achieve 5,000 monthly website visitors and 3,000 social followers.

    Key Activities:
    • Launch subscription portal, Stripe integration
    • Expand content: templates, modules, compliance
    • Execute marketing campaigns and webinars
    • Onboard and support first 100 customers
    Milestone: 100 paid users, $50,000 revenue, 8,000+ audience reach

  4. Phase 4 Months 19‑36

    Expansion

    Scale to 500+ customers, expand to food processing and chemical manufacturing sectors, and grow to 50 templates and 20 training modules. Secure 10 vendor partnerships, $100,000 in supplier fees, and $100,000 in grants. Begin international expansion pilots and contribute to industry research.

    Key Activities:
    • Expand to new sectors and international pilots
    • Grow content, predictive maintenance, analytics
    • Secure vendor/association partnerships
    • Contribute to industry research and standards
    Milestone: 500+ users, $200,000+ revenue, global pilot, industry recognition
